Our Story

More than just clothing.

Raagi was created from a simple belief: getting dressed should never become another obstacle in your day.

Many people with ADHD experience sensory discomfort, distracting textures, restrictive fits, and decision fatigue from overly complicated wardrobes.

We are building clothing that feels predictable, comfortable, and easy to wear — so your attention can stay where it belongs.

Raagi is not simply clothing. It is comfort designed with understanding.
